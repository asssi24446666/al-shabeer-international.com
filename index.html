Our History

Since 1957, AL-SHABEER INTERNATIONAL has been a leading name in the sheep casing industry.

Founded by Mr. Shabeer Bhutta**, Back to 1957. Our founder, Mr.Shabeer Bhutta, a visionary entrepreneur, ventured into the natural sheep casing business with unwavering determination and perseverance. Despite facing numerous challenges and setbacks, he successfully established a reputable presence in the local market, built on the principles of quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction _Early Years (1957-1980)_ In the early years, Mr. Shabeer Bhutta worked tirelessly to build a loyal customer base, focusing on providing exceptional products and services. His dedication and hard work paid off, as the company grew steadily, earning a reputation for excellence in the local market. _Expansion and Growth (1980-1997)_ As the company continued to thrive, Mr. Shabeer Bhutta expanded his operations, investing in new processes to enhance efficiency and productivity. This strategic move enabled the company to meet the growing demand for natural sheep casings, further solidifying its position in the market..

In 1997, Mr. Pervaiz Bhutta** A New Era of Leadership (1997-2018) In 1997, Mr. Pervaiz Bhutta, the current owner and son of our founder, took the reins of the business. Under his visionary leadership, the company experienced unprecedented growth, driven by innovative marketing strategies, strategic partnerships, and a commitment to sustainability. During this period, the company expanded its product range, invested in research and development, and explored new markets.

Since 2018, International Expansion In 2018, the third generation of the Mr, Shabeer Bhutta family joined the business, bringing fresh ideas and a global perspective. Our company, Al- Shabeer International, was founded, and we expanded our operations to reach international markets. This milestone marked a significant turning point in our history, as we began to share our high- quality natural sheep casings with customers worldwide. Today, we are proud to serve a diverse customer base, spanning across continents. _A Legacy of Quality and Excellence_ Throughout our journey, we have remained committed to our core values of quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. Our dedication to excellence has earned us a loyal customer base, recognition in the industry, and numerous awards and accolades. We continue to build on the legacy of our founder, Mr. Shabeer Bhutta, and strive to make a lasting impact in the global market
